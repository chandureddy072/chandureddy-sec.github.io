About Me

I'm Chandu Reddy, a cybersecurity student from Hyderabad, Telangana. My path into security started with a deep curiosity about how systems work — and more importantly, how they can be broken.

I'm currently enrolled in the Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing program at Craw Security Institute, where I've built a strong foundation in Linux, networking, and Python before moving into offensive security techniques.

I believe real skill comes from hands-on practice — not just watching tutorials. Every lab I build, every CTF I attempt, and every writeup I document is a step toward becoming a professional penetration tester.

My goal is to earn the OSCP certification and work as a red team operator or penetration tester at a security firm.
